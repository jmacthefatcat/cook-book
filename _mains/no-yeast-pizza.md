---
title: No Yeast Pizza
layout: recipe
category: mains
picture: no-yeast-pizza
ingredients:
- 2 cups flour
- 2 tsp of baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 3/4 cup of water
- 1/4 cup of olive oil
- whatever you want for toppings
steps:
- preheat oven to 425
- mix dry ingredients
- stir in water with a wooden spoon
- stir in olive oil
- knead
- split in half
- flatten both into pizza crusts
