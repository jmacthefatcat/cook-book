---
title: Spinach and Artichoke Dip
layout: recipe
category: sauces
picture: null
ingredients:
- 1 bundle of spinach, chopped
- 1 jar of artichokes, chopped
- 1 - 8oz brick of cream cheese
- 1/2 cup of sour cream
- 1 cup shredded cheese
- Pitas, chips or crackers for serving
steps:
- Blend together cream cheese and sour cream, artichokes and spinach.
- Fold in half of shredded cheese.
- Spread into 8x8 glass dish and top with remaining shredded cheese.
- Bake 20-30 minutes, at 350 degrees or until cheese is melted.
- Serve with pita triangles, chips or crackers.
---

Best served warm!
