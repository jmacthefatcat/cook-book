---
title: Granola
layout: recipe
category: snacks
picture: null
ingredients:
- 1 cup almonds
- 1 cup brazil nuts
- 1 cup cashews
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1 cup dates
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup hemp hearts
- maple syrup
- coconut oil
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
steps:
- Mix all contents together.
- Coat with maple syrup and coconut oil, sprinkle cinnamon and nutmeg.
- Bake in oven until golden.
