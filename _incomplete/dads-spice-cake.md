- 1/2 tsp of Nutmeg
- 1/4 tsp of Cloves
- 1 cup of thick Sour Cream

- Cream shortening
- Add sugar, eggs, and salt
- Add flour, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves
- Once fully mixed add sour cream
- Bake for 50 minutes
