---
layout: recipe
title: Mac and Cheese
category: mains
picture: stirfry-sauce.jpg
ingredients:
 - 2 cups of Macoroni
 - 1/4 cup of Butter
 - 1/4 of Flour
 - 3 cups of Milk
 - 3 cups of Shredded Cheese
steps:
 - Melt butter, add flour, and a pinch of salt
 - Gradually add Milk
 - Cook and stir until thickened
 - Add Cheese, stir until completely melted
 - Add cooked macoroni
