---
title: Spice Cake
layout: recipe
category: snacks
picture: null
ingredients:
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp nutmeg
- 1/4 tsp cloves
- 1 cup thick sour cream
steps:
- Cream shortening, add everything together EXCEPT sour cream.
- Add Sour cream in last. Mix together then bake at 350 for 50 minutes.
