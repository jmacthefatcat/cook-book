ingredients:
 - 2/3 cup Soy Sauce
 - 1/2 cup Chicken Broth
 - Lemon Juice to taste
 - 1 tbsp garlic powder
 - 1 tbsp ground ginger
 - 2 tbsp of Cornstarch
steps:
 - Whisk together
 - Add Cornstarch slowly to desired thickness
