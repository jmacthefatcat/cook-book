---
title: French Onion Soup
picture: french-soup
ingredients:
- 6 Onions
- 2 Garlic Cloves
- 5 Sprigs of Thyme
- 5 Bay Leaves
- 100g Butter
- 25ml Sherry
- 100ml Red Wine
- 1.5L Beef Stock
steps:
- Sweat Onions in butter Garlic, thyme, and bay until caramalized.
- Season.
- Add Sherry, wine, and cook au sec.
- Add beef stock and simmer 30 mins
- Season
---
