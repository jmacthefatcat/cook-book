---
title: Curry Salad Dressing
category: sauces
picture: curry-dressing
ingredients:
- 2/3 cups olive oil
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 1/2 tbsps curry powder
- 2 tsp dijon mustard
- 1/2 tsp of salt
steps:
- Combine everything and blend until smooth and emulsified
