---
title: Curry Salad Dressing
category: sauces
picture: null
ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cups olive oil
- 1 cup maple syrup
- 1 cup apple cider vinegar
- 2 tsp cumin
- 2 tbsp curry powder
- 2 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp salt
steps:
- Combine everything and blend until smooth and emulsified
---

For a quick salad use red onion, apples, chicken tossed in dressing. Lay on lettuce, add cheese and walnuts. Topped with dressing.
