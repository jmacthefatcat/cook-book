---
title: Coleslaw
layout: recipe
category: snacks
picture: coleslaw
ingredients:
- 1 Cabbage
- 1 Carrot
- 1 tbsp of Celery Seed
- 1/2 cup of White Sugar
- 1 cup of Vinegar
- 2 tbsp of Olive Oil
- 1 tbsp of Salt
steps:
- Chop up Cabbage
- Shred/Julian Carrots
- Mix everything together
---

You can also add thinly sliced Onion.
